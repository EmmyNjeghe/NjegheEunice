Clownfish

  • Scientific Name: Amphiprioninae
  • Average Length: 11 Centimetres
  • Average LifeSpan: 7 years
  • Habitat: Tropical Coral Reefs

Bright orange with three distinctive white bars, clown anemone fish are among the most recognizable of reef-dwellers. They reach about 11 centimetres in length, and are named for the multicolored sea anemone in which they make their homes.
